About Us




Who we are


Crossbones Cafe is a small business partnered with Skullz Bar to allow students to take part in Structured Workplacement Learning and Work Experience to give people the opportunity to serve and prepare drinks to customers where ever it may take them!

We were founded in 2025 to help people grow their barista skills and to help them develop their Work History here for their resume and to make delicious coffee!!!



Our Mission


The main focus of Crossbones Cafe is to not only serve our customers great food and drinks, but to also help those studying in either culinary, hospitality or both to develop their skills so they can prepare and serve goods to customers when they start their careers.
